Shimano

Ultegra Di2 R8150

This Ultegra Di2 R8150 Road groupset from Shimano consists of a 2x12-speed crank with 36-52-tooth gradation, a flat-mount disc brake set and an electronic rear derailleur and front derailleur for semi-wireless shifting. Of course, a rechargeable battery, a Quick-Link chain and a cassette with 11-30 or 11-34 teeth are also included.

Specifications

Series Ultegra Di2 R8150
Application Road
Shifters & Derailleurs: 2-speed, 12-speed (electronic, semi-wireless)
Brakes disc, hydraulic (flat mount)

Components
