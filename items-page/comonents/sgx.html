SRAM

GX Eagle AXS

This SRAM upgrade kit of the GX Eagle AXS series comes directly with a cassette and offers a low-priced option for an electronic 1x12-speed drivetrain on your mountain bike. It includes a GX Eagle AXS rear derailleur with battery, an Eagle AXS controller shifter, an XG-1275 cassette, a PC X01 Eagle chain as well as a battery charger and a mini-tool.
