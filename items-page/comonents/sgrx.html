Shimano

GRX RX600

With the GRX RX600 1x11 groupset, Shimano does not reinvent the Gravel scene, but equips it with components designed specifically for riding on non-paved roads. So that you and your friends can be exactly where and how you want to be. The groupset consists of a 1x crank with a 40-tooth chainring, rear derailleur, disc brake with STI right shift lever, chain and cassette (105 or MTB). A great package for cycling adventures, bikepacking tours or off-road races.

Specifications

Series GRX RX600
Application Gravel, Cyclocross, City & Touring
Shifters & Derailleurs: 11-speed (mechanical)
Brakes disc, hydraulic (flat mount)

Components

Crank 170.0 mm, 172.5 mm, 175.0 mm (40 teeth)
Cassette 11-30, 11-32, 11-34, 11-40, 11-42
Front Derailleur braze-on
Rear Derailleur short (mid) cage (11-30, 11-32, 11-34), long cage (11-40, 11-42)
