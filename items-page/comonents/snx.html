NX Eagle

Eagle™ technology has revolutionised mountain biking. Designed as a specific 1-speed drivetrain, it is lightweight, robust, intuitive to use and has the right gear range for any terrain. The NX Eagle™ drivetrain is the latest addition to the Eagle 1-speed ecosystem, and because it shares technologies with the XX1, X01 and GX Eagle groupsets, it is also 100% compatible within the Eagle ecosystem. NX Eagle makes it easier than ever to join the Eagle Ecosystem - so more riders can experience the performance of Eagle.
