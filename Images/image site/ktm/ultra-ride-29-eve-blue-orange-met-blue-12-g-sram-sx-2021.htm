A Relationship You Can Trust. Featuring a new improved frame for 2021 the ULTRA builds on its outstanding reputation. The strong and extra light aluminium frame offers perfect performance for everyday life and on the trails. We have lengthened the top tube and slackened the head angle to make this Ultra the most stable, fastest, and fun one yet. Integrated cables improve the bikes aesthetics. The cheaper ULTRAs do not have a thru axle, but as always, they are ready for mounting kickstand, rack, and mudguards. Some of the models are also available in 27,5-inch version. With the ULTRA you definitely have a wide range of use, without blowing up your piggy bank.
