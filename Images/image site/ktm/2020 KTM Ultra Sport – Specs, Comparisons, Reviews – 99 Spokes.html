KTM Ultra EVO DIM review

Luke Marshall

A fun-handling bike, but a few spec choices and limited sizing stop it from reaching its thrill-seeking potential

  • Mullet wheel setup gives fun handling
  • Up-to-date head and seat tube angles
  • Plenty of frame details that add versatility
  • Limited sizing
  • Reach is on the short side and the seat tube quite tall, making it harder, to size up
  • SRAM’s SX Eagle gears are underwhelming
